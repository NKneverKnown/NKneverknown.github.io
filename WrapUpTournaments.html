Upcoming Tournaments

Our chess club hosts regular tournaments for players of all skill levels. Whether you're just starting out or have been playing for years, we have events that will challenge and entertain you.

Monthly Club Tournament

Date: First Saturday of each month
Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Location: Club Meeting Room
Entry Fee: $5 for members, $10 for non-members
Prizes: Trophies for top 3 players

Our monthly tournament is open to all players. We divide participants into skill-based groups so everyone has a fair chance. Each player will get at least 4 games during the tournament.

Beginner's Tournament

Date: Third Sunday of each month
Time: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
Location: Club Meeting Room
Entry Fee: Free for members, $5 for non-members

Specifically designed for players who are new to chess or still learning the basics. We'll have experienced players available to answer questions and provide tips between games.

Tournament winners receive these beautiful trophies

Annual Championship

Date: November 15-16, 2025
Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM both days
Location: Community Center Main Hall
Entry Fee: $20 for members, $30 for non-members
Prizes: $500 first prize, $300 second prize, $200 third prize

Our biggest event of the year! This two-day tournament attracts players from across the region. Players will be divided into sections based on rating, with prizes in each section. There will also be food vendors and chess merchandise available.

How to Register

To register for any tournament, please email chessclub@example.com with:

  • Your name
  • Contact information
  • Which tournament you want to enter
  • Your estimated skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
